About

I am currently pursuing a PhD degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I'm very fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Yue Chen. Prior to this, I earned my Master's degree and Bachelor's degree in CUHK-Shenzhen, where I collaborated with Prof. Junhua Zhao.

Currently, I work at the nexus of smart grid technology, AI, and optimization techniques.
